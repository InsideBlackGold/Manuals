- Tips on the Iraq

  
                   

 
 
 
                   
  - Use several  ammunition transporters for your supply depot. Delivery is  much faster.

- Play World War III using groups. This  gives you a better overview and understanding. If you want to create a group, number 1 for example, select few units than press "1" and the CTRL key together. The group icon will pop up on the screen with number of the goup and quantity of units in group. If you're looking for this group, press "1" key or click on group icon and that group will be automatically highlighted. Press the "1" key again or click on group icon again, and the camera will focus in on the group.

- You can also transport airborne units back and forth between your Main Base and the Mission Area (first Russian campaign only).

- Double click on a unit to select all units of that type.

- Use the "Print Screen" key to make screen-shots and save them in the installed directory as a .bmp file.

- If game speed problems occur, use the "Option" settings and lower graphic details. And you could also try not  zooming too far away from the scene.

- You can mouse-scroll through Text windows (like mission briefings). Just press right mouse button and move the mouse backwards and forwards.

- During a game pause (Pause key), you can still give commands.

- Use your space bar to jump to a unit or structure mentioned in the last base message. For example:

"We are under Attack" - > SPACE > displays the unit being attacked.

- The "." key on the number block  turns the camera northwards.

- You can configure all keyboard commands using "Options/Keyboard".

- You can delete previously saved games in the "Save Game" window. Highlight the one you want to delete and press the Backspace key.

  You can save / record multiple commands and also simple waypoints using the following procedure:

Press the "R" key and give your commands (e.g., attack unit, repair unit, move). Then press the "X" key to "play them back".

Waypoint system:

To set a route for a unit, press the "R" key. Now click on the waypoints in the order in which you want your units to go to them. Press "Z" key ("Repeat key"). Press the "X" key and the unit(s) will continually go to these waypoints.

Use the "CAPS LOCK" key to set several destinations, one after the other, for your units. This is similiar to uing "R" key with one difference. Units will start executing commands immediately (will not wait for X key).

  Use Camcopter units to recognize the map terrain, and spy on enemy units and bases. Camcopter is small, cheep and  fast, but unfortunately very weak, and easy to shoot down.

Americans are specialists in electronic warfare, so they have another nice toy - the Jammer. This device mounted on  vehicles, or built into bases causes interference to enemy communication systems - they lose control (player or AI).

NATO allies can lend US troops their powerfull Leclerc tank  , and you can order this unit during the game (of course. when available). Leclerc is expensive, but a  very good investment. There is no such thing as a problem that can't be solved using a platoon of Leclercs together with air support.

On the US. tanks you can mount flareslaunchers, it is good protection against enemy helicopters.

  The Russians have a chemical weapon in their arsenal , use BM 21 with chemical rockets to kill the crews of enemy units, after that you can capture their weapon using assault helicopter Ka-29.

To discover maps and spy on the enemy use Spy Center.

Remember that every Russian unit automatically recharges its HP when not carrying out other commands. If your battle units have really taken a beating, take them out of the combat zone and leave them somewhere safe for a while - the Russian soldiers will automatically repair their vehicle.

Most of Russian units are amphibious, so they can go through the water.

Russian Robm units (flak mobile artillery) are pretty good, and their upgraded rockets ignore enemy flares, that means - one shot, one death.

  Iraqi units have a camouflage net, which they can use to hide with, and  use  surprise tactics. Camouflaged units are immobille and can be revealed only by  radar, or units which are very close (2 squares).

The Long Range Artillery buildings have unique range and fire power.

Iraqi rocket artillery (Astros) is very effective and dangerous.

Devastator units (kamikaze) may raze everything standing in their way to the ground.

Jeeps with TOW = great weapon against tanks.
