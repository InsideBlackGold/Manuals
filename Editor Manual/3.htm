1. We recommend that you set your starting points near oil resources. It can be pretty annoying if you have to look all over the place for your starting points later!
  2. You have to keep in mind just how fast your computer is when you're adding additional items like trees, bushes, ruins, civilian buildings, etc. The more of these you add, the slower the game will be.
  3. Save the game area often. This way you won't lose any of your changes.
  4. Remember to leave enough room for the construction of your base.
  5. Just as in real life, don't build your structures on sand or cliffs!
  6. On generated or modeled terrain use noise generation, it makes your map more realistic
  7. Use ALT + G combination to show the waynodes net, this net shows you passable terrain. The parts of the map which are beyond the net are inaccessible for ground units and cannot be built upon
